COMMUNITY

The open source community is a global ecosystem of developers, driven by passion and expertise. They believe in transparency, collaboration, and inclusivity, creating software and projects accessible to all. They foster cutting-edge technologies, encourage knowledge sharing, and empower individuals to contribute to the digital world.

CONTRIBUTE

We welcome and encourage contributions to our open source GitHub projects, and we wholeheartedly accept pull requests in your branch. Our commitment to an inclusive and collaborative development environment means that we value the diverse perspectives and expertise that each contributor brings to the table. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, we believe in providing opportunities for individuals to make meaningful contributions and have their ideas heard. By accepting pull requests in your branch, we aim to empower you to take ownership of your contributions and ensure that your work aligns with your vision. Together, we can build upon the collective knowledge and skills of the open source community to create innovative and impactful solutions that benefit users worldwide. Join us in making a difference through open collaboration and help shape the future of our projects.

PROJECTS TO DO

Our focus is on developing secure communication tools, including a Video Web Conference and Encrypted group messaging web application and other projects. We invite developers to contribute by submitting pull requests, fostering a collaborative spirit and diverse perspectives. Our goal is to create innovative solutions that empower users while ensuring security. Join us in shaping the future of communication technology.
